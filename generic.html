About Me

My name is Nikita Jha. I was born in India, and I am currently a sophmore at Northview High School. Some of my hobbies including coding, swimming, playing piano, rock climbing, and playing table tennis. I also love to be an active member of my community and volunteer at the temple. I started coding when I was in elementary school; however, I became passionate for the field when I started high school. I enrolled in my school's AP Computer Science course and and also joined many clubs including FBLA, HOSA, and programming club. I did not want to join robotics, because I wanted enough time to learn advanced programming on my own and create some applications and projects to showcase my talent. My dreams are to start my own business and use my coding to help people all over the world.
