Flash Heart

Reinventing the way you monitor your heart's health

Here is an image of the outside of the prototype:

My detection system relies on the testing of four critical cardiac biomarkers: FABP3, Creatine Kinase, Myoglobin, and Troponin. In my prototype, I used the Arduino Uno, a photoelectric cell, and an AddaFruit Touch Screen Display in order to stimulate shining light through a blood sample to detect for various cardiac biomarkers.

Shown below is the results pane of the prototype. Based on the light that filters through the blood sample, a corresponding output will be displayed: Normal, Precuation, or Danger.

Shown below is a diagram of the entire theory of the prototype, which I constructed before building the entire model. The diagram depicts the placement and set up of every piece of the actual model in order to accurately depict the detection system.
