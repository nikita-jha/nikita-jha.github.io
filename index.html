My Projects

Cardiac Biomarker Detection Software

Heart Attacks are the number one cause of death in the United States, accounting for approxiametly 25% of deaths on an annual basis. For this project, I created a prototype that detects light coming from an external source to model light going through a blood sample. This represents a detection kit based off of cardiac biomarkers including FABP3, Myoglobin, Creatine Kinase, and Troponin.

FlashHeart

This project consists of a touchscreen heart attack monitor prototype. In order to update and revamp my previous heart attack monitor prototype (listed on the side), I created a touchscreen Arduino based circuit board with a photoelectric cell as well as an AMOLED AdaFruit display. The device is an all in one, self-sustaining, heart attack monitor, which can be used in the real world for early detection of heart attacks. Unlike the previous device, it is much more compact and does not require an outside energy source.
